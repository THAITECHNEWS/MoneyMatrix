## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Start Small:** Focus on Priority 1 cities first, then scale
2. **Quality Over Quantity:** Better to rank 30 cities well than 300 cities poorly
3. **Be Patient:** SEO takes 6-12 months - don't expect overnight results
4. **Track Everything:** Data-driven decisions beat guesswork
5. **Stay Consistent:** Regular updates beat sporadic big pushes
6. **Focus on Users:** Content that helps users ranks better
7. **Local First:** Genuine local relevance beats generic content

## üö® Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. ‚ùå **Duplicate Content:** Don't copy-paste content between cities
2. ‚ùå **Keyword Stuffing:** Natural content ranks better
3. ‚ùå **Ignoring Mobile:** Most searches are mobile - optimize for mobile
4. ‚ùå **Slow Pages:** Page speed matters - optimize images, code
5. ‚ùå **No Updates:** Stale content doesn't rank - update regularly
6. ‚ùå **Poor Internal Linking:** Help Google understand your site structure
7. ‚ùå **Ignoring Analytics:** Track what works and double down
