### 3. **Content Patterns That Work**

#### Pattern 1: Branded + Product Combinations
- `[brand] credit card` - Very low KD (0-4), high volume
- Examples: academy credit card, maurices credit card, llbean mastercard
- **Action:** Create pages for every major store/brand credit card

#### Pattern 2: Tax & Financial Terms
- `[tax term]` - KD 1-10, high informational intent
- Examples: oasdi tax, backup withholding, qbi deduction
- **Action:** Create comprehensive tax glossary and guides

#### Pattern 3: "How Much" Questions
- `how much is [product/service]` - KD 6-10, high commercial intent
- Examples: how much is youtube tv, how much is hello fresh, how much is homeowners insurance
- **Action:** Create cost comparison pages for popular services/products

#### Pattern 4: "Best Time to Buy" Content
- `best time to buy [product]` - KD 1-4, seasonal traffic spikes
- Examples: best time to buy a mattress, best time to buy a tv, best time to buy furniture
- **Action:** Create seasonal shopping guides with calendar-based content

#### Pattern 5: Comparison Content
- `[product] vs [product]` - KD 1-4, very low competition
- Examples: amex points vs chase points, passport card vs book, hostel vs hotel
- **Action:** Create comparison tables and detailed "vs" content
