# Prompt Template C: Listicle & Comparison Format

You are a financial content expert at MoneyMatrix.me. Write an engaging, list-based 1000-word article about "{topic}" in the {category} category that helps readers make informed decisions.

## Article Requirements:

### Structure:
- Use semantic HTML: `<article>`, `<section>`, `<h1>`, `<h2>`, `<h3>`, `<table>`, `<strong>`, `<aside>`
- Open with compelling statistics or surprising facts (100-150 words)
- Present 7-10 main points as numbered list items
- Include detailed explanations for each point (80-120 words each)
- Conclude with a summary and next steps

### Content Elements:
- Feature 1 comprehensive comparison table with ratings or scores
- Insert 3 relevant images: `[IMAGE: comparison-filename.jpg]`
- Add "What to Look For" criteria section
- Include "Red Flags to Avoid" warnings
- Create a "Bottom Line" summary box

### SEO Focus:
- Target keyword: "{topic}"
- Use numbered list keywords ("best", "top", "compare")
- Include year-specific terms (2025, current, latest)
- Optimize for featured snippet potential

### Linking Strategy:
- Embed 2 natural internal links:
  1. Money page link: {money_page_url} within relevant context
  2. Related article link: {related_article_url} for additional reading
- Use descriptive anchor text that adds context

### Content Style:
- Use data-driven approach with statistics
- Include rankings or ratings where appropriate
- Add "Why It Matters" explanations
- Use transitional phrases between list items
- Maintain objective, research-based tone

### Article Framework:
```html
<article>
<h1>{topic}</h1>

<section>
<p>Hook with interesting statistic or trend...</p>
<p>Overview of what readers will discover...</p>
</section>

<section>
<h2>1. [First Main Point]</h2>
<p>Detailed explanation with specific examples...</p>
<h3>Why This Matters</h3>
<p>Impact and relevance to readers...</p>
</section>

[Continue numbered sections...]

<section>
<h2>Comparison Table</h2>
<table>
<thead>
<tr><th>Feature</th><th>Option A</th><th>Option B</th><th>Option C</th></tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
[Table rows...]
</tbody>
</table>
</section>

<aside>
<h3>Key Takeaway</h3>
<p>Most important insight from the comparison...</p>
</aside>

<section>
<h2>Making Your Decision</h2>
<p>Summary and recommendation framework...</p>
</section>

</article>
```

Create content that serves as a definitive resource readers will bookmark and share. Focus on providing clear comparisons and actionable rankings that simplify complex financial decisions.
